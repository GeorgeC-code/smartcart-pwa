## Full Description
SmartCart is your ultimate companion for grocery shopping and budget management. Designed with a privacy-first approach, SmartCart helps you stay within your budget while managing multiple shopping lists with ease.

**Key Features:**
- **Real-Time Budgeting:** See exactly how much you're spending as you add items to your cart.
- **Premium Experience:** Enjoy an ad-free experience with our symbolic 2 USD one-time upgrade.
- **Privacy First:** Your data never leaves your device. No cloud indexing, no tracking—just your shopping.
- **Smart Statistics:** Visualize your spending habits with detailed charts and historical data.
- **Multi-Language Support:** Available in English, German (Deutsch), Spanish (Español), and Russian (Русский).
- **Haptic Feedback:** Experience micro-vibrations for a more intuitive tactile experience.

**Why Choose SmartCart?**
Unlike other apps, SmartCart is built on top of a philosophy of simplicity and security. We don't store your data on servers, and we don't track your behavior. It's just a powerful, beautiful tool to help you save money and shop smarter.
