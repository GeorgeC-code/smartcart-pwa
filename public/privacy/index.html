Privacy Policy for SmartCart

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

Our Commitment: SmartCart is built with a strict privacy-first philosophy. Your shopping lists and personal spending history belong to you, and we store them purely on your own device.

SmartCart (the "Service", "App", "We") is published as a free tool to help users manage grocery shopping lists and budget requirements offline. This Service is designed to operate safely, securely, and privately. This document informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Local Storage Only (No Cloud Database)

All items, expenses, store names, budget configurations, multi-language settings, and historical logs you input into SmartCart are stored exclusively in your device's local memory (via local cache, index DB, or standard client-side browser storage).

  • We do not operate a remote server database for cloud syncing your files.
  • We cannot view, index, monitor, or access what you search for, what items you register in your shopping trips, or your budget limits.
  • Your data is as secure as your physical device. If you delete the App or clear your browser data, your history will be permanently deleted since we run zero remote backups.

2. Third-Party Integration & AI Prompts

SmartCart includes an optional modern generative feature (the Showcase menu) that integrates with the Google Gemini API to produce smart visual assets, receipt previews, or icon mockups based on your interactive requests.

When you trigger this feature, the descriptive prompt text you enter is sent securely to Google Gemini API servers. No account details, emails, trackers, or cookies are collected or bundled with these calls. We highly advise against inserting any personally identifiable information (PII) inside your custom showcase descriptions.

3. No User Tracking or Third-Party Analytics

We do not use cookies, pixels, or profiling trackers to identify you. No Google Analytics, Facebook SDKs, or advertising network SDKs are loaded inside this application, ensuring an ad-free flow and absolute privacy for your grocery trends.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our Service is intended for use by Everyone and does not collect any data. Since we collect exactly zero personal information from any of our corporate or individual users, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect or store files from children under the age of 13. SmartCart is safe to operate for all ages.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may upgrade or polish our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will publish any edits on this page so they are easily auditable. We advise you to review this page periodically for any adjustments.
